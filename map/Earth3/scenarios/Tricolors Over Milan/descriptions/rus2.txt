The Russian Empire is a vast and imposing power, still basking in the afterglow of victory over Napoleon. Tsar Alexander the II, notable for his foreign pacifist policy, has in plan some radical reforms, such as the emancipation of Russia's serfs, reorganizing the judicial system, setting up elected local judges, abolishing corporal punishment, promoting local self-government through the zemstvo system, imposing universal military service, ending some privileges of the nobility, and promoting university education. Will these promised reforms ever come to fruition? Only time will tell...
